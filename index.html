Last Updated: April 22, 2026

BeenThere (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the BeenThere mobile application, website (beentheretrips.com), and related services (the “Services”).

A. Information You Provide

We may collect:

  • Email address and account login information
  • Profile details you choose to provide
  • Messages, feedback, or support requests
  • Content you create or upload

B. Location Data (Important)

BeenThere is a location-based app, and we may collect:

  • Precise GPS location (when enabled)
  • Visit and movement data related to locations you interact with
  • Location-based activity such as badges, stamps, rewards, and trips

This data is used to power core features of the app. Disabling location services may limit functionality.

C. Automatically Collected Information

We may collect:

  • Device information (device type, OS, app version)

If you make purchases, we may collect:

  • Transaction details

Payments are processed by third-party providers. We do not store full payment card details.

If you interact with ads, including rewarded ads, third-party partners may collect limited data to deliver ads, measure performance, and prevent fraud.

We use information to:

  • Create and manage accounts
  • Process purchases and rewards
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Detect fraud, abuse, and security issues
  • Communicate with users
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies, SDKs, and similar technologies for authentication, analytics, performance, saving preferences, and advertising where applicable.

You can control some tracking through your device or browser settings.

5. How We Share Information

We may share information with:

  • Service providers such as hosting, analytics, payments, and support providers

  • Legal authorities if required by law
  • Business transfer parties in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale

We do not sell your personal information for money.

6. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as necessary to provide the Services, maintain security, and meet legal obligations.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Request a copy of your data

If you live in certain U.S. states, including California, you may have additional rights under applicable laws. We do not discriminate against users for exercising their privacy rights.

11. Children’s Privacy

BeenThere is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

12. Changes to This Policy

Delete Your BeenThere Account

This page explains how BeenThere users can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to request account deletion

  1. Email beenthere.reach@gmail.com .
  2. Use the subject line: BeenThere Account Deletion Request.
  3. Include the email address associated with your BeenThere account.
Request Account Deletion

What will be deleted

When your deletion request is processed, we will delete your BeenThere account and associated personal data, including:

  • Account profile information
  • App activity tied to your account
  • Location-based app records associated with your account
  • User-submitted support messages tied to your account, where applicable

What may be retained

We may retain limited information when required for legal, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or regulatory compliance purposes.

Retention period

We aim to process deletion requests within a reasonable period after verifying the request. Some limited retained data may be stored only for as long as legally required or reasonably necessary for security and compliance.

Questions

If you have questions about account deletion, contact beenthere.reach@gmail.com .
