Last updated: April 8, 2026

BeenThere (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when using the app.

  • To provide, maintain, and improve BeenThere

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

BeenThere is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
